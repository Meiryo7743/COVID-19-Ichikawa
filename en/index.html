Website has been closed

2021-02-01

This website has been stopped updating and closed as of January 31st, 2021 (JST). I would like to express my deep gratitude to all those who have used it in the short period of less than a year since its opening.

Instead of this site, you can get the latest information on COVID-19 and its related from Ichikawa City official website, Chiba Prefecture official website, and so on. However, you will have to read these pages through a translator because, almost all cases, they provide the detailed information about COVID-19 in Japanese only. The number of passengers on Toei Subway is accessible on Tokyo Metropolitan Government COVID-19 Information Website.

The source code of the site is still available on GitHub, but I will do neither maintenance nor accepting issues and pull requests.

If you have any questions, feel free to ask me on the contact form. Please note that it will be closed on March 31st, 2021 (JST).
